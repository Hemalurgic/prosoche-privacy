Privacy Policy — Prosoche: Stoic Focus Timer

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Prosoche is a focus timer app. We do not collect personal information, require account registration, or operate any servers that store your data.

Data stored on your device

The following is stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to us:

  • Focus session history and streak data
  • App settings and preferences
  • Premium subscription status

Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data including app opens, session length, and feature usage. This data cannot be used to identify you personally. You can opt out via your device's advertising settings.

In-app purchases

Payments are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store any payment or billing information.

Notifications

If you grant permission, we send daily Stoic quotes and streak reminders. You can disable these at any time in your device settings.

Third-party services

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google Play Billing

Children

This app is intended for users aged 18 and over and is not directed at children.
